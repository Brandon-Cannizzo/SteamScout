What is SteamScout?

At this time it is not complete, however SteamScout will be a streamlit app where you can search for and compare PC games and their prices across various launchers and vendors

Possibly helpful resources

python-steam-api documentation: https://pypi.org/project/python-steam-api/

Steam WebAPI documentation: https://partner.steamgames.com/doc/webapi

Endpoint: https://store.steampowered.com/api/appdetails?appids={APP_ID}
Key Art Fields:
header_image: Main banner used at the top of the store page.
screenshots: An array of URLs for high-resolution in-game screenshots.
background: Full-page background art.
